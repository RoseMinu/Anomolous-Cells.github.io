Who we are

The Anomalous Cells initiative is a collaboration between energy storage engineers, data scientists, and safety operations specialists. We combine telemetry insights with domain expertise to protect customers and extend the lifespan of energy assets.

Data Science

Designs the detection algorithms, maintains feature stores, and evaluates performance.

Engineering

Owns telemetry integration, firmware updates, and validation rigs for lab testing.

Safety Ops

Translates alerts into field actions and ensures compliance across global regions.
