Exploring behavioural signals

Lead analyst: Priya Chen
Status: In progress

Objectives

The exploratory phase seeks to understand how voltage sag, temperature spread, charge cycles, and contextual telemetry interact. Findings here inform feature engineering, data cleaning, and early hypotheses for model design.

  • Quantify normal vs abnormal ranges for key sensor readings.
  • Expose correlations between environmental conditions and performance.
  • Identify patterns that precede flagged anomalies in historical incidents.

Interim learnings

Highlights so far

  • Cells with >5°C deviation from pack median show 3× higher anomaly scores.
  • Voltage sag events cluster around rapid charge ramps in colder climates.
  • Ambient humidity correlates with increased impedance variability.
