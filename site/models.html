Building anomaly detection models

Experiment tracker: MLflow workspace #42
Validation window: Jan–Mar 2025

Strategy

We layer statistical heuristics with machine learning models to capture both interpretable thresholds and complex multivariate behaviour. The stack is designed for transparency so operations teams can trace why a cell was flagged.

Baselines

EWMA control limits and z-score rules establish immediate guardrails.

Machine learning

LSTM autoencoders and isolation forests compare residuals across sensors.

Explainability

SHAP summaries and counterfactuals provide actionable narratives.

Pipeline architecture

Each candidate model is evaluated in weekly batches with stratified sampling across pack families. We track precision at top-k anomalies alongside recall of historic failure events to balance efficiency and safety.
