Telemetry snapshot

Battery telemetry arrives from connected vehicles and stationary storage packs. Each record represents a one-minute interval aligned to a specific pack, string, and cell identifier. Measurements are streamed via secure MQTT brokers before landing in the analytics warehouse for modelling.

Acquisition flow

Raw telemetry is buffered on edge devices, validated for schema compliance, and forwarded to the warehouse where derived aggregates such as rolling deltas and cell-to-pack ratios are computed.
