Key findings

Insight 1

Thermal gradients lead anomalies

Temperature variance is the strongest lead indicator, preceding voltage anomalies by ~45 minutes.

Insight 2

Charging behaviour matters

Rapid charge sessions at low ambient temperatures drive most false positives in current baselines.

Insight 3

Explainability builds trust

Service teams prefer SHAP narratives that point to specific sensor deviations per alert.
